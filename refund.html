Refund Policy

No Returns Accepted. All sales are final.

These covers are made to order. I do not accept returns. I highly recommend purchasing a fabric sample prior to ordering a cover to ensure the color and quality of the fabric.

At Kings Road, all of our individually handmade pillow covers are custom made-to-order specifically for you, we do not carry inventory. Each item is crafted once a customer has placed an order in our studio. Due to the fact that our pillow shop handcrafts custom-made pillow covers, we do not offer refunds, returns or exchanges. We strongly encourage customers to place a fabric swatch order prior to purchasing our pillow covers to ensure the color / texture will work with the décor. Fabric samples are final sale.
